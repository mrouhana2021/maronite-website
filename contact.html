COMMON QUESTIONS

Quick Answers

Can non-Maronites attend your liturgies?

Absolutely. All are welcome to attend our liturgies and events. Catholics of any rite may receive Holy Communion; guests of other traditions are warmly invited to attend and observe.

How do I become a member of the Association?

Simply fill out the contact form above and select "Membership / Join Us." We'll reach out with details about membership and upcoming events.

Do you offer sacramental services such as baptism or marriage?

Yes. Please contact us and we will connect you with the appropriate clergy for sacramental preparation and scheduling.

Do you have programs for youth and families?

Yes — we offer faith formation, cultural programs, and social events for all ages. Contact us to learn more about our current offerings for youth and families.
