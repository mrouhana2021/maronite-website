Feast of Saint Maron

MARIAN FEAST

Our Lady of Lebanon Celebration

A special Mass and gathering honoring Our Lady of Lebanon — patroness of the Maronite faithful. Festive dinner and cultural entertainment to follow.

Exaltation of the Holy Cross
