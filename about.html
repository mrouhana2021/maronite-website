OUR MISSION

Who We Are

The Maronite Association of British Columbia serves as a spiritual and cultural home for Maronite Catholics across the province, preserving our ancient heritage while building a vibrant community.

Our Community

Founded by Lebanese Maronite families who settled in British Columbia, our association has grown into a welcoming community that spans generations. We celebrate our Syriac liturgical tradition — one of the oldest living Christian rites — while embracing all who seek spiritual nourishment and fellowship.

Whether you are a lifelong Maronite, a newcomer to British Columbia, or someone curious about our ancient faith, you are warmly welcomed among us.
